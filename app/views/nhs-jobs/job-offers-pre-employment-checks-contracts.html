You can then make a job offer to another applicant.

You can create a contract template using the NHS Jobs service. If you do not want to use the service, you can create a contract outside of the service before sending it to applicants.

The contract template online options are an addendum to an existing contract, a contract of employment, an internal secondment and external secondment. If you choose online, a 'Super user' must have created the contract template first.
