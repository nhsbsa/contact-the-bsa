{% endset %} {% set preEmployment %}

If pre-employment checks are being completed online, you must complete the following:

  • references
  • home address

If the job vacancy needs the details of your professional registration, you'll also need to provide this using the NHS jobs service.

You must complete the following checks outside of the NHS Jobs service:

  • identity check
  • right to work in UK
  • qualifications
  • occupational health check

You'll also need to provide details of Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) and Healthcare Professional Alert Notices (HPANs) outside of the NHS Jobs service if they are needed.

You must provide documents as evidence of your pre-employment checks with the employer.

You're unable to upload any pre-employment documents directly to the NHS Jobs service. The employer will contact you to let you know how to provide your documents.

If you have any questions about pre-employment documents, contact the employer.

You can find their contact details on the right hand side of the vacancy advert.

Further support and user guides are available on our website.

For bitesize video tutorials, subscribe to our NHS Jobs YouTube channel. Select the bell icon for future notifications.
