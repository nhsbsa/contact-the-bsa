CCS 'To Be' Version
USER RESEARCH

User research

Awaiting user need and user research recommendation

Changes from previous iteration

Solicited

  • Building on previous drafts, the entry point for the solicited service will come via a notification sent by CCS (email, letter or SMS).
  • Email and SMS will contain a link to the specific point in the journey which will allow the citizen to enter their reference number, name, date of birth and address. Once supplied, they can upload their requested documents.
  • Letter should contain a short link to the root of the service which will allow them to select which service they are contacting us about, what they want to do (upload a document or evidence) and supply their personal details and upload their requested documents.

Unsolicited

  • Building on previous drafts, the entry point for the unsolicited service will come via each service page on the NHSBSA website (a start page is also available if users come via another source or service variable isn't found in the URL).
  • After selecting which service they wish to contact us about, users are then given 3 options:
    1. General query
    2. Change name, date of birth or address
    3. Upload document or evidence
  • Updated routing questions based on Content Design team feedback (1/11/2023)
